Case study — CPG leader

98% utilisation.
Costs and carbon cut simultaneously.

The client situation

Rising fuel costs, a global driver shortage, and trucks running below capacity. 90–95% utilisation sounds efficient — until you're shipping hundreds of loads daily across a global network. That 3–8% gap compounds into extra trucks, extra miles, extra emissions, and extra cost — every single day.

The outcome

AutoO2 pushed utilisation to 98%. Transport costs down 4–8%. Carbon emissions cut 4–8%. Net-zero target supported through 2039. Efficiency and sustainability turned out not to be in conflict.

Real impact — Unilever scale

4,675 acres
of forest. Every year.

Why this number matters

285,000 tons of CO₂ reduced. 88,000 trucks eliminated per year. The carbon sequestered by 4,675 acres of US forest — eliminated by AutoO2 across the network. This is what happens when you close the utilisation gap at Unilever scale.

How to show impact without a case study NDA

The numbers are modelled from 21-day client impact data, annualised. Specific enough to be credible. Structured so the buyer can translate it directly to their own operation. Every metric on the page is answerable with a single question: "what would this look like for us?"
