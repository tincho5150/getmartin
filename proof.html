Supply chain · Interactive explainer

Show them the math
everyone else avoids.

The problem

Load optimisation software is invisible by definition — it works inside systems buyers can't see. The value was real but abstract. Prospects couldn't connect the software to the savings.

What we built

A real-time 3D truck loading visualiser that shows the optimizer working live. Three load scenarios — beverage, CPG mixed, food and beverage. The buyer watches the algorithm pack their truck in real time, sees the utilisation percentage climb, and runs their own ROI calculation against their actual freight spend. A network flow simulator shows what happens when a promotion spike hits — and how LevelLoad absorbs it.

What it changes

The abstract becomes concrete. They've seen their truck get packed before the demo. The question shifts from "does this work?" to "when can we start?"
